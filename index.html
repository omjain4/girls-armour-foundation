Girls' Armour Foundation

We are dedicated to the holistic empowerment and welfare of women through various initiatives that address key issues affecting their lives. Our core objectives focus on enhancing women’s education and providing livelihood support, promoting women’s rights, and spreading awareness about menstrual health. We are committed to aiding women in overcoming exploitation, injustice, and corruption while ensuring they receive essential support in times of need.

Through our programs, we aim to educate women on menstrual health, providing necessary hygiene kits and food items to underprivileged communities. Additionally, we offer free professional counseling to women facing challenges such as emotional distress, sexual harassment, violence, and cyberbullying, ensuring they have a safe space and access to the help they deserve.

Our Mission

"Our mission is to empower and uplift women by providing education, livelihood support, and advocating for their rights. We aim to address critical issues such as menstrual health, exploitation, and injustice while offering essential resources and guidance to those in need. Through our initiatives, we strive to create a supportive environment where women can overcome challenges, gain confidence, and lead dignified lives, free from discrimination and harm."

Our Vision

"Our vision is to build a society where every woman is empowered, respected, and has equal opportunities to thrive. We envision a world where women are free from exploitation, injustice, and inequality, with access to education, health, and support systems that allow them to live with dignity and confidence. By fostering awareness and creating lasting change, we aim to create communities where women’s rights are upheld, their voices are heard, and they are fully equipped to realize their full potential."

Our Initiatives

GEM (Girls' Empower Meet)* by the Girls' Armour Foundation is a culturally-focused program that provides a platform for girls to showcase their talents in various art forms, including dance, music, drama, and other cultural performances. The initiative celebrates creativity and encourages self-expression while fostering a sense of community and confidence among participants. By offering this stage, the foundation aims to empower young girls to embrace their cultural heritage, build self-esteem, and explore their artistic potential in a supportive environment. GEM is designed to recognize and nurture the unique talents of each girl, promoting inclusivity and empowerment through the arts.
