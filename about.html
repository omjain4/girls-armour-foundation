Our Mission

The Girls Armour Foundation is dedicated to improving the lives of girls and young women across the globe. We focus on providing the necessary tools and resources to help girls reach their full potential. Our efforts include distributing essential sanitary products like pads, providing educational resources, and offering mentorship programs.

Our Work

We are committed to breaking down barriers that prevent girls from accessing education and essential healthcare. Every month, we distribute thousands of sanitary pads to girls in need, ensuring they can attend school without interruption. We also publish a monthly magazine that highlights the stories of young women making a difference in their communities, inspiring others to do the same.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every girl is empowered to achieve her dreams, where she is free from discrimination and inequality. Through our initiatives, we aim to create a future where girls are confident, educated, and able to lead the way in their communities.
